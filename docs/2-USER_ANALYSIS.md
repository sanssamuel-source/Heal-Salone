# HEAL Salone - User Analysis

## 4. User Personas

### 1. Aminata (The Rural Mother)

- **Profile:** 28 years old, pregnant, lives in a village outside Makeni. Limited internet access.
- **Pain Point:** scared of complications, closest clinic is 10 miles away, doesn't know if her symptoms are urgent.
- **Goal:** Needs immediate, trustworthy advice without traveling.
- **Platform Use:** Uses "AI Assistant" for symptom check; gets reassurance or distinct instruction to go to hospital.

### 2. Nurse Fatmata (The Local Caregiver)

- **Profile:** 40 years old, Community Health Officer (CHO) at a peripheral health unit. Overworked.
- **Pain Point:** overwhelmed by paper records, misses outbreak trends until too late.
- **Goal:** Digital tools to log patient aggregated data and alert Freetown of supplies shortage.
- **Platform Use:** Uses "Report Issue" to log daily stats and "Supply Request".

### 3. Samuel (The Freetown Youth Reporter)

- **Profile:** 22 years old, university student, tech-savvy activist.
- **Pain Point:** Sees open sewage/unsafe water in his community but has no "official" channel to complain.
- **Goal:** To improve his community's hygiene standards.
- **Platform Use:** Takes photos of unsafe water sources, uploads to "Report Issue", tracks status on Dashboard.

### 4. Mr. Kamara (Ministry Data Officer)

- **Profile:** 50 years old, Ministry of Health HQ in Freetown.
- **Pain Point:** Making decisions based on data that is 2 weeks old.
- **Goal:** Real-time situational awareness.
- **Platform Use:** Uses "Admin Analytics Dashboard" to watch report heatmaps and dispatch resources.

---

## 5. User Journey Flow (Aminata's Case)

1.  **Onboarding:** Aminata opens HEAL Salone on her husband's phone. One-click "Get Started".
2.  **Triage:** She selects "AI Assistant" and types/speaks: "My baby is not moving much today."
3.  **Assessment:** AI analyzes the query against medical guidelines and responds: "Reduced movement can be serious. Please visit the clinic immediately." maps the nearest center.
4.  **Reporting:** The system automatically logs an anonymized "Maternal Risk" event in the backend.
5.  **Action:** The Dashboard updates. Mr. Kamara sees a spike in "Maternal Risk" in Makeni region.
6.  **Follow-up:** Aminata returns from the clinic and presses "I got help", closing the loop.
